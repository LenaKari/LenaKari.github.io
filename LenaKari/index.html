About

Olena is a freelance developer and software consultant who splits her time between Edinburgh and Berlin. She is also the CEO and Co-Founder of she.codes, the social coding school for women. Her poems and paintings have appeared in print, she has provided musical direction for several stage productions, she has composed several small-ensemble pieces, and she is passionate about making the world a better place.
