[ARRA]STRE

ROLE: DEVELOPER

ABOUT THE PROJECT

[arra]stre was a digital data driven dance performance that derived its movements and concepts from computer science theory. The performance broke down certain theories and concepts through the movement of ballet. Olena created a responsive Bootstrap site from scratch for [arra]stre, which involved streaming the event live and embedding data visualisations via Codepen.
